# FORGE — VISION.md

## Vision Statement

**Forge is the AI Builder Operating System** — the platform that teaches anyone to build real products with AI agents, automatically adapting education and workflow to their progress.

We are not a chatbot. We are not a course platform. We are not another IDE.

We are the operating system for the next generation of builders.

---

## The World We See

By 2027, 80% of new digital products will involve AI agents in their creation. Yet **95% of people with ideas cannot build them.**

Not because they lack intelligence. Because the tools are fragmented:
- One tool for learning
- Another for coding
- Another for project management
- Another for deployment
- Another for AI prompts

**No system connects idea → skill → product → market.**

Forge does.

## The Forge Loop

```
Idea → Goal Detection → Build Roadmap → Agent Team Assignment
  → Building → Weakness Detection → Just-in-Time Learning
    → Product Progress → Skill Growth → Repeat
```

This loop runs automatically. The user doesn't need to know what to learn.
Forge detects what they need and teaches it at the right moment.
