# FORGE — MISSION.md

## Mission Statement

**Democratize product building by combining AI agents, adaptive learning, and real-world project workflows into one operating system — bilingual from day one.**

---

## What We Do

Forge turns anyone into a builder. Not in 6 months of tutorials. In days of actual building.

We combine three things nobody else has combined:

### 1. Adaptive Learning Engine
Not static courses. A system that:
- Maps skills to your project needs in real-time
- Detects knowledge gaps as you build
- Delivers lessons exactly when needed (just-in-time learning)
- Adjusts difficulty based on your progress
- Tracks skill growth across projects

**Result**: You learn what matters, when it matters. No wasted time.

### 2. Agent-Assisted Building
Not a chatbot. An orchestrated AI workforce:
- **18+ specialized agents** collaborate on your project
- Each agent has a role: Builder, Frontend, Backend, Debugger, Mentor, Architect, Deployer...
- You see the plan, the progress, the next steps
- The system explains decisions and teaches as it builds
- Real code. Real files. Real products.

**Result**: You ship faster than a solo developer — with a whole team behind you.

### 3. Mentorship Model
Not just code generation. Real guidance:
- The Mentor agent explains every concept you encounter
- The Romanian agent (RO) teaches in your language
- Lessons are injected into the build workflow — not separated in a course tab
- Daily progress tracking, not just "Chapter 3 of 12"
- You build a portfolio, not just complete exercises

**Result**: You grow as a builder, not just as a student.

---

## How It Works (The User Journey)

1. **You describe an idea** — "I want to build a SaaS tool for habit tracking"
2. **Forge creates your roadmap** — AI agents generate project structure, tech spec, MVP scope
3. **Agents start building** — Real code, real files, real tests. You watch and learn.
4. **Forge detects gaps** — "You haven't worked with Stripe before. Quick lesson: 2 minutes."
5. **You ship** — Deploy to production. Your product. Your users. Your revenue.

This is the mentorship model: **teach at the moment of need**, not at the moment the curriculum says so.

## Bilingual: English + Romanian

Forge is built in Romania, for the world.

- **English** — primary language for global reach
- **Romanian** — native support from day one, not an afterthought

- Cultural adaptation, not just translation

**Why**: The best builders don't only speak English. EdTech shouldn't either.
